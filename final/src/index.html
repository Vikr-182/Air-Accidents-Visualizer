Air crashes

This web page provides an extensive visualisation of the air crashes that took place from 2000 till march 2020.
Just adjacent is a zoomable globe which can be rotated. It acts as a book shelf visualisation where you can hover through any link on the globe to get all its details and select the year via dropdown.

A scatter plot representing fatalities vs damage vs phase Of an accident

The radius of the circle represents the total number of deaths involved in the accident. The colour represents the damage of the aircraft, the darker the colout the hugher being the damage. A node is placed in the column representing the phase in which the crash happened.
From this we conclude that most accidents take place in the landing phase of the flight while the most fatal and damaging ones happen En route.
